About Zikar Flow

Zikar Flow is dedicated to providing top-notch Quranic education to students worldwide. Our mission is to ensure that everyone—regardless of location or background—can learn the Quran with accuracy and understanding. We offer flexible online classes, one-on-one sessions, and a wide range of courses to meet each student's unique needs.

Muhammad Abbas

Muhammad Abbas is the founder and instructor at Zikar Flow. With years of experience teaching students in Pakistan and abroad, he specializes in making Quran learning accessible, engaging, and highly effective. His passion for sharing knowledge and nurturing spiritual growth has helped countless students achieve their Quranic goals.

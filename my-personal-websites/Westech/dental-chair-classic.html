Product Features of Classic

Doctor’s Unit+

  • Flexible swing over-patient well balanced arm for easy positioning.
  • Holder type instrumentation.
  • Large size instrument tray.
  • 3 tubing’s and fitting for instruments.
  • Main On/Off Control with indicator.
  • 3-Way syringe (Quick Connect / Dis-connect) autoclaveable nozzle.
  • X-Ray Illuminator.
  • Individual Water & Air pressure controls.
  • Rectangular HP pressure indicator.
  • Foot controller.

Main Control Panel+

  • Soft touch membrane switches Easy to clean on Doctor and Assistant side.
  • Chair preset controls.
  • Chair manual controls.
  • Chair Auto-return control.
  • Cup filler Control.
  • Bowl Flush Control.
  • X-Ray Illuminator Control.
  • Dental Light Control.
  • Display window indicating Function in use.

Cuspidor Unit+

  • Soft touch membrane switch panel for assistant.
  • Chair/unit dental light complete control panel.
  • Streamline Housing for easy cleaning.
  • One piece spittoon with solid collector Bowl Flush & Cupfiller.
  • Automatic cupfiller Bowl Flush, adjustable time cycle control.
  • Bowl Flush adjustable time, cycle control.
  • 3 way syringe, quick connect/disconnect autoclaveable nozzle.
  • Saliva Ejector autoclaveable hand piece with suction pressure adjustment valve.
  • High Volume evacuator with autoclaveable hand piece with suction.

Junction (Utility) Box Built-in Base+

  • Air Water Inlets with filters.
  • Service water outlet.
  • Separate Electrical compartment.
  • Voltage input 240/220V 50/60Hz.
  • Output voltage 24 V-AC.

Dental Light (LED)+

  • Programmable Dental light with chair movement and pre-set positions + Touch On/Off at Dr. and Assistant control panel.
  • The Dental Light is compact, well balanced and easy to move to any position.
  • Excellent light focusing pattern for patient convenience.
  • Daylight shadowless and heatless light for optimal results.
  • Dental light is very easy to keep hygienically clean.
  • This Dental light is compatible with all Dental Systems.
  • Input 240/220V.
  • Light On/Off and intensity can be controlled by hand sensing.

Dental Chair+

  • Fully Automatic Hydraulic Dental Chair.
  • Four pre-set positions.
  • Preset positions programmable by user.
  • One Touch Zero return.
  • Excellent safety features such as no chair movement when any hand piece in use.
  • Synchronized seat tilting with backrest movement.
  • Back Rest movement synchronizes human anatomy, so no patient sliding or discomfort while changing from sitting to operating or operating to sitting position.
  • Anatomical contoured seamless upholstery.
  • Swing out Arm rest.
  • Double axis fully Articulating Headrest for adult and children forward, Backward, Upward, and Downward movements.
  • Motor Pump Capacity 8L/M silent.
  • Hydraulic Fluid capacity 1000ml approx.
  • Solenoid Valve Assembly 12V AC.
  • Joy Stick Foot Control for chair movement.
  • Upholstery color selection from Shade Card.

Safety Features+

  • Electric 12V Limit switches to cut off Auto/manual movements at maximum/minimum positions.
  • Automatic safety oil drain to reservoir system in case of excessive pressure in cylinder.
  • All Chair controls operate on 12V, AC.
  • Push of any Manual/Unit foot switch override the Automatic movements and stops chair immediately.
  • Once chair is obstructed by any object during auto movements, it stops instantly.
  • Input: AC-240/220, 50/60 HZ.

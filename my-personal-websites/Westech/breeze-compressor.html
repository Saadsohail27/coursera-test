Breeze Compressor description

Product Features of Breeze Compressor

Oil Free Dental Air Compressor
Model “BREEZE” with Silencer Cabinet

  • Tank made of M.S Sheet 5 SWG.
  • Double Layer welding with Drain plug and pressure gauge.
  • Painted with durable Head Treated paint.
  • Air shut OFF valve.
  • Safety valve release.
  • Flush valve for tank cleaning.
  • Adjustable pressure regulator with gauge and air filter and water trap.
  • Efficient and Trouble Free.

Specifications

  • Intake: 100 LPM
  • Motor type: ¾ h/p
  • Noise Level: 55 dB with silencer cabinet
  • Working Pressure: 5-8 Bar
  • Tank Capacity: 25 Ltr
  • Dimensions: 35 (H) × 16-1/2 (D) × 37 (L) inches
  • Weight: 34 Kg. Approx
  • Voltage: 220/240 Volts 50/60 Hz. Single Phase

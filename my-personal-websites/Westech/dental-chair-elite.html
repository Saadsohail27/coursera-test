    Product Details

    Dental Chair

    • Fully Automatic Hydraulic Dental Chair.
    • Four pre-set positions
    • Preset positions programmable by user.
    • One Touch Zero return.
    • Excellent safety features such as no chair movement when any hand piece in use.
    • Synchronized seat tilting with backrest movement.
    • Back Rest movement synchronizes human anatomy, so no patient sliding or discomfort while charging from sitting to operating or operating to sitting position.
    • Anatomical contoured seamless upholstery.
    • Swing out Arm rest.
    • Double axis fully Articulating Headrest for adult and children forward, Backward, Upward and Downward movements.
    • Motor Pump Capacity 8L/M silent.
    • Hydraulic Fluid capacity 1000ml approx.
    • Solenoid Valve Assembly 12V AC.
    • Joy Stick Foot Control for chair movement.
    • Upholstery color selection from Shade Card.

      Doctor’s Unit

    • Over arm tray hand piece lay out 6 longer than standard
    • Soft touch membrane switches on Dr & Assistant side facilitating user all controls of unit and chair within easy reach
    • Four preset selectable position for multi group users, one gargling & one zero return.
    • Microprocessor control system programmable cup filler, bowl flush and dental light etc
    • Conveniently located 4-5 Rod a type hand piece provides long reach
    • Excellent maneuverability while keeping the pressure off form dentist wrist by its self-balance system also protecting
    • Prevention of accidental fall down of Hand pieces
    • Extra Large instruments tray specially designed under dentist element can be positioned for easy access during surgery
    • OPG X-Ray film view and 17” LED Screen Display
    • Ergonomically designed oval shape assistant element has excellent look and easy wipe clean sterilization, built in gravity drain
    • Separate, extendable bar mounted, high vacuum & low vacuum suction for 2/4 handed dentistry
    • Autoclave able quick disconnects system
    • Quick connectable tip 3 way syringe
    • Inlet water and air filters with supplies cut off control to facilitate reliable trouble free service and separate electrical compartment in junction box built in the chair base eliminating pipes etc

      Dental Light (LED)

    • Programmable Dental light with chair movement and pre-set positions + Touch On/Off at Dr. and Assistant control panel.
    • The Dental Light is compact, well balanced and easy to move to any position
    • Excellent light focusing pattern for patient convenience
    • Day light shadow less and heatless light for optimal results
    • Dental light is very easy to keep hygienically clean
    • This Dental light is compatible with all Dental Systems
    • Input 240/220V
    • Light On/Off and intensity can be control by hand sensing

    Safety Features

  • Electric 12V Limit switches to cut off Auto/manual movements at maximum/minimum positions.
  • Automatic safety oil drain to reservoir system in case of excessive pressure in cylinder.
  • All Chair controls operate on 12V, AC.
  • Push of any Manual/Unit foot switch override the Automatic movements and stops chair immediately.
  • Once chair is obstructed by any object during auto movements, it stops instantly.
  • Input: AC-240/220, 50/60 HZ.

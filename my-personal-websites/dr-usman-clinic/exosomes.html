What is Exosome Therapy?

Exosome therapy is a cutting-edge treatment that uses nano-sized vesicles derived from stem cells to deliver powerful growth factors and proteins to the skin and scalp. This helps rejuvenate skin, reduce aging signs, and stimulate hair regrowth.

  • Skin Rejuvenation: Improves skin elasticity, reduces wrinkles, and enhances skin tone and texture.
  • Hair Restoration: Stimulates dormant hair follicles, encouraging natural hair growth and improving density.

What happens during the procedure?

The treatment involves applying or injecting exosomes directly into the targeted area. Local anesthesia may be used for comfort. The exosomes deliver potent growth factors that stimulate repair, regeneration, and rejuvenation of the skin or scalp. The procedure is safe, quick, and requires minimal downtime.

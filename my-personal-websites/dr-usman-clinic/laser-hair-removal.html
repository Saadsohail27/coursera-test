What is Laser Hair Removal?

Laser Hair Removal uses advanced laser technology to target and destroy hair follicles, reducing hair growth. This procedure is ideal for anyone looking for a long-term solution to unwanted hair.

  • How it Works: The laser emits light that is absorbed by the pigment in the hair follicles, destroying them without harming the surrounding skin.
  • Customizable Treatment: The procedure can be tailored to different skin types and hair colors for optimal results.

What happens during the procedure?

The area to be treated is cleansed, and a cooling gel may be applied. A laser device is then used to deliver targeted energy to the hair follicles. The treatment is quick, with most sessions lasting 15-60 minutes depending on the area. Slight redness may occur, but it subsides quickly.

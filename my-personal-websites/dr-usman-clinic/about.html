Committed to Excellence

Dr. Usman Azhar is a board-certified hair restoration surgeon from the prestigious American Board Of Hair Restoration Surgery. With over 10 years of experience in the field of hair restoration and aesthetic medicine, he is committed to delivering exceptional care and outstanding results to his patients.

As an MBBS gold medalist from FMH/UHS and holding permanent membership of Hair Restoration society of Pakistan Dr. Usman combines his extensive knowledge and expertise to bring the latest advances in hair surgery and aesthetics to his patients. He believes in the power of technology and medicine working hand in hand, and is proud to make cutting-edge advancements accessible to all.

Whether you’re seeking solutions for skin concerns, hair restoration, hair fall reduction or aesthetic enhancements, Dr. Usman and his dedicated team are here to provide personalized treatments tailored to your unique needs. With a passion for excellence and a commitment to patient satisfaction, Dr. Usman strives to help individuals achieve their desired aesthetic goals and boost their confidence.

Experience the difference of exceptional care and embrace the transformative journey to healthy, strong hair, radiant skin and enhanced aesthetics at Dr. Usman’s clinics.

Our Story

Dr. Usman’s clinical journey commenced in 2014 when he underwent a hair transplant himself. This personal experience led him to realize the significant impact of baldness on both men and women. It can severely diminish a person’s confidence, potentially leading to serious mental health issues like anxiety and depression.

Initially, Dr. Usman honed his skills at a local clinic in Lahore, where he gained valuable experience in the field. In 2019, he achieved a prestigious milestone by becoming a Diplomat of the esteemed American Board of Hair Restoration Surgery.

Expanding his expertise, Dr. Usman began practicing aesthetics alongside hair restoration at various renowned clinics in Lahore. His dedication and exceptional skills soon earned him recognition, leading to the establishment of his clinic in the heart of Gulberg Lahore, at MM Alam Road.

The overwhelming support from his valued patients fueled Dr. Usman’s passion for innovation. He embarked on research work in both aesthetics and hair restoration, publishing numerous research papers and papers for publication. To date, six research papers have been published.

Dr. Usman takes immense pride in providing high-quality services that meet the highest standards of excellence. His clinics are equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and staffed by a dedicated team committed to delivering exceptional care in a safe and secure environment.

What is Mesotherapy?

Mesotherapy is a technique that involves micro-injections of a custom blend of nutrients, vitamins, and active ingredients into the middle layer of the skin (mesoderm). It can be used for facial rejuvenation, reducing pigmentation, treating hair loss, and improving overall skin health.

  • Facial Mesotherapy: Focused on hydrating and brightening the skin, reducing wrinkles, and improving skin texture.
  • Scalp Mesotherapy: Stimulates hair follicles, improves blood circulation, and promotes hair regrowth for thinning or damaged hair.

What happens during the procedure?

The procedure involves applying a numbing cream to ensure comfort. A fine needle is used to deliver the customized solution into the targeted areas. The treatment is quick and relatively painless, with results visible after multiple sessions.

Benefits of Dermal Fillers

Restore Volume

Dermal Fillers help replenish lost volume in areas such as cheeks, lips, and under the eyes.

Enhance Contours

Define and sculpt facial features, creating a more balanced and symmetrical look.

Smooth Fine Lines

Effectively reduces the appearance of wrinkles, creases, and folds for smoother skin.

Long-Lasting Results

Depending on the filler used, results can last from 6 months to over a year.

What are Dermal Fillers?

Dermal Fillers are injectable treatments that use gel-like substances to rejuvenate and enhance facial aesthetics. These fillers are commonly used to address aging concerns, improve skin elasticity, and boost overall facial volume.

  • Hyaluronic Acid Fillers: The most popular type, these fillers hydrate and plump the skin, providing natural-looking results.
  • Calcium-Based Fillers: Provide structural support and stimulate collagen production for firmer, youthful skin.

What happens during the procedure?

The area is cleansed, and a numbing cream may be applied for comfort. Using fine needles, the filler is injected into the targeted areas. The procedure typically takes 20-30 minutes, and results are immediate, with minimal swelling or redness.

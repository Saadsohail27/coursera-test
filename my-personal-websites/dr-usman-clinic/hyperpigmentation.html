What is Hyperpigmentation Treatment?

Hyperpigmentation treatments are designed to reduce the appearance of melanin deposits in the skin, which cause dark patches and uneven tones. These treatments are suitable for individuals looking to address age spots, sun damage, and melasma.

  • Chemical Peels: Exfoliates the top layer of skin to reveal a brighter, more even complexion.
  • Laser Therapy: Targets and breaks down pigmentation for a smoother, clearer skin tone.
  • Topical Treatments: Uses medical-grade serums and creams to reduce pigmentation and prevent recurrence.

What happens during the procedure?

The treatment begins with a skin consultation to determine the best approach. Depending on the method, the procedure may involve exfoliation, application of specialized products, or targeted laser therapy. These treatments are safe, effective, and require little to no downtime.

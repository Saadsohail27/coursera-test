What is a Thread Lift?

Thread Lift is a cosmetic procedure that uses dissolvable threads to lift and reposition sagging skin, while also encouraging collagen production to enhance skin firmness and texture. It is ideal for treating aging signs like sagging cheeks, jowls, or loose skin on the neck.

  • Barbed Threads: Designed to lift and hold skin in place for a firmer, tighter appearance.
  • Smooth Threads: Stimulate collagen production and improve fine lines, wrinkles, and overall skin texture.

What happens during the procedure?

The procedure begins with local anesthesia to ensure comfort. Special threads are inserted into the skin using a fine needle, which are then tightened to lift the skin and enhance facial contours. The process is quick, safe, and requires minimal recovery time.

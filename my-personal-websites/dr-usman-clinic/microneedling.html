What is Microneedling?

Microneedling is a cosmetic procedure that involves using fine needles to create micro-injuries in the skin. These controlled injuries trigger the body’s natural healing response, boosting collagen and elastin production for a more youthful appearance.

  • Skin Rejuvenation: Treats fine lines, wrinkles, and acne scars, improving overall skin quality.
  • Enhanced Product Absorption: Opens micro-channels that allow skincare products to penetrate deeper for maximum effectiveness.

What happens during the procedure?

After applying a numbing cream, a microneedling device is used to create controlled micro-injuries on the skin’s surface. The treatment is safe and typically takes 30-60 minutes, depending on the targeted areas. Mild redness and sensitivity are common, but recovery is quick, and results improve over time.

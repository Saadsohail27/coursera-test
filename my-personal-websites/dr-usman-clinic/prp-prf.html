What is PRP/PRF Therapy?

PRP and PRF are regenerative treatments that use the healing properties of your blood to rejuvenate the skin, stimulate hair growth, and address signs of aging. These therapies involve drawing a small amount of blood, processing it to concentrate the platelets and growth factors, and injecting it into targeted areas.

  • PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma): Rich in growth factors, PRP stimulates collagen and accelerates tissue repair, making it ideal for skin rejuvenation and hair restoration.
  • PRF (Platelet-Rich Fibrin): Contains a higher concentration of growth factors and fibrin, offering longer-lasting results compared to PRP.

What happens during the procedure?

A small amount of blood is drawn from the patient, which is then processed to extract PRP/PRF. The concentrated solution is injected into the treatment areas, such as the scalp for hair growth or the skin for rejuvenation. The procedure is safe, quick, and has minimal downtime.

American Board of Hair Restoration Surgery (ABHRS)

The American Board of Hair Restoration Surgery (ABHRS) is recognized worldwide as one of the most prestigious certification boards in the field of hair restoration. Earning ABHRS certification signifies a surgeon’s dedication to the highest standards of professional excellence, ethical conduct, and patient care.

Why ABHRS Certification Matters

ABHRS certification assures patients that their surgeon has undergone rigorous training, met strict competency requirements, and continually stays at the forefront of hair restoration innovations. By choosing an ABHRS-certified professional, you can feel confident in receiving state-of-the-art treatments and excellent patient care.

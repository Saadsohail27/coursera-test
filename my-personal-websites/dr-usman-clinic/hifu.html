What is HIFU?

HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) uses ultrasound waves to penetrate deep layers of the skin, tightening and lifting it by stimulating collagen production. It is ideal for reducing wrinkles, tightening loose skin, and contouring the face and neck.

  • Facial Lifting: Effectively lifts the cheeks, jawline, and brows for a rejuvenated look.
  • Neck Tightening: Reduces sagging and improves skin elasticity in the neck area.
  • Wrinkle Reduction: Smoothens fine lines and wrinkles for a more youthful appearance.

What happens during the procedure?

The treatment begins with a consultation to determine target areas. Ultrasound gel is applied to the skin, and the HIFU device is used to deliver focused energy to deep skin layers. The process is non-invasive, safe, and requires no downtime, making it convenient for busy lifestyles.

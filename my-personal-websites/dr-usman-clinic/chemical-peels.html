What are Chemical Peels?

Chemical Peels involve the application of a specially formulated solution to exfoliate and rejuvenate the skin. This treatment is ideal for addressing uneven texture, fine lines, and pigmentation issues.

  • Superficial Peels: Targets the outer layer of skin to address minor blemishes and improve tone.
  • Medium Peels: Penetrates deeper layers for treating wrinkles, acne scars, and pigmentation.
  • Deep Peels: Provides intensive treatment for significant skin concerns, offering long-lasting results.

What happens during the procedure?

After cleansing, a chemical solution is applied to the skin. The peel works to exfoliate the damaged layers, which will eventually flake off, revealing fresh, healthy skin. The procedure is safe, effective, and tailored to your skin's needs.

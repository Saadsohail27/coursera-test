What are Clinical Facials?

Clinical facials are medical-grade skin treatments performed by skincare professionals. These facials go beyond regular spa treatments, using advanced techniques and products to target skin concerns and enhance your skin’s health and appearance.

  • Exfoliation: Removes dead skin cells to reveal a brighter, smoother complexion.
  • Hydration Treatments: Replenishes moisture levels to restore skin elasticity and softness.
  • Targeted Masks: Addresses specific issues like acne, sensitivity, or aging signs.

What happens during the procedure?

A thorough consultation is conducted to understand your skin's needs. The facial involves cleansing, exfoliation, targeted treatments, and hydration. Each step is customized to ensure optimal results, leaving your skin revitalized and glowing. The procedure is gentle, relaxing, and requires no downtime.

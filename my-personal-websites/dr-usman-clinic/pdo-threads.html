What are PDO Threads for Hair Restoration?

PDO Threads are biodegradable polydioxanone threads inserted into the scalp to stimulate hair follicles, enhance blood circulation, and promote natural hair growth. This minimally invasive procedure helps improve thinning hair and overall scalp health.

  • Mono Threads: Thin, dissolvable threads that boost collagen production and improve blood circulation in the scalp.
  • Screw Threads: Twisted threads designed to create a stronger lifting effect and stimulate dormant hair follicles.

What happens during the procedure?

Local anesthesia is applied to the scalp to ensure comfort. PDO Threads are then inserted using fine needles to stimulate collagen production and blood circulation around the hair follicles. The procedure is quick, safe, and requires minimal downtime.

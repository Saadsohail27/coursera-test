Benefits of PDO Threads

Non-Surgical Solution

PDO Threads offer a safe, non-invasive alternative to surgical facelifts for lifting and tightening sagging skin.

Collagen Stimulation

Promotes natural collagen production, which enhances skin elasticity, texture, and overall appearance over time.

Customizable Treatment

The treatment can be tailored to specific areas, such as the cheeks, jawline, neck, or brows, to achieve personalized results.

Natural and Long-Lasting Effects

The results improve gradually as the threads dissolve, leaving behind tighter and rejuvenated skin.

What are PDO Threads?

PDO Threads are dissolvable polydioxanone threads inserted into the skin to lift and tighten sagging areas while encouraging natural collagen production. This treatment is ideal for addressing aging signs, such as wrinkles and loss of firmness.

  • Barbed Threads: Designed to provide a lifting effect, these threads anchor the skin and reposition it for a firmer appearance.
  • Smooth Threads: Stimulate collagen production to improve fine lines, wrinkles, and skin texture.

What happens during the procedure?

Local anesthesia is applied to ensure comfort. A fine needle is used to insert the threads into targeted areas of the skin. The threads lift the skin and stimulate collagen production, resulting in a rejuvenated and youthful look. The procedure is quick, safe, and minimally invasive, requiring little downtime.

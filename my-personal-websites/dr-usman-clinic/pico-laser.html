What is Pico Laser?

Pico Laser is a non-invasive, high-energy laser treatment that uses ultra-short pulses to target pigmentation, acne scars, and other skin imperfections. This advanced technology promotes collagen production and rejuvenates the skin for a more youthful appearance.

  • Pigmentation Removal: Breaks down melanin into tiny particles, helping to reduce dark spots and uneven tone.
  • Scar Reduction: Improves acne scars and other textural irregularities by promoting skin regeneration.
  • Skin Rejuvenation: Stimulates collagen for firmer, smoother, and more radiant skin.

What happens during the procedure?

The treatment begins with cleansing the targeted area. The Pico Laser device delivers ultra-short pulses of energy to the skin to break down pigments or stimulate collagen. The procedure is quick, gentle, and suitable for all skin types, with minimal discomfort.

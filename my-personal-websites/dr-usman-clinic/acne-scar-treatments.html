What are Acne Scar Treatments?

Acne scar treatments involve a variety of techniques to diminish the appearance of scars left by past breakouts. These treatments are designed to enhance skin texture and tone, addressing both physical and emotional concerns related to acne scarring.

  • Microneedling: Stimulates collagen production to reduce the depth of scars and improve skin texture.
  • Laser Therapy: Targets scarred areas to resurface the skin and promote natural healing.
  • Chemical Peels: Exfoliates the skin to remove damaged layers and encourage the growth of new, healthy skin.

What happens during the procedure?

The procedure depends on the treatment method chosen. For example, microneedling involves using tiny needles to stimulate the skin’s natural repair process, while laser therapy focuses on precisely targeting scarred areas. Each session is quick, minimally invasive, and tailored to your skin's needs.

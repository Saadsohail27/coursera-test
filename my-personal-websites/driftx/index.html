Who We Are

DriftX is a team of 5 enthusiastic students participating in Formula Pakistan competition for F1 in schools. We are committed to pushing the boundaries of motorsport while creating a positive social impact. Our mission extends beyond just the racetrack — we race to support education for underprivileged students and empower the next generation of Pakistani talent.

Through engineering excellence, teamwork, and a vision for a better future, DriftX continues to prove that motorsport can drive real change.

How aligner treatment works at Dentistica

Scan → Digital plan → Staged aligners → Review

Treatment begins with a digital scan and a tailored treatment plan showing the predicted tooth movements. We provide a series of clear trays worn in stages, with periodic reviews to track progress and refine treatment when needed.

1 — Consultation & Scan

Initial assessment, photos and a 3D scan to evaluate suitability and capture current tooth positions.

2 — Digital Treatment Plan

We design a step-by-step movement plan and show the expected outcome before manufacturing trays.

3 — Aligners & Monitoring

Patients wear aligners ~20–22 hours/day and change trays per schedule; periodic reviews ensure movement remains on track.

Benefits
  • Almost invisible during wear
  • Removable for eating and hygiene
  • Digital predictability and fewer emergency visits
Am I a candidate for aligners?

Many mild-to-moderate cases are suitable. We’ll determine candidacy during the consultation and scan.

How long does treatment take?

Typical treatment ranges from several months to around 18 months depending on complexity; we'll provide an estimate after planning.

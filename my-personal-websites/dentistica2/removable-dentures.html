Removable Dentures

Custom removable dentures designed and finished for comfort, function and natural aesthetics. We combine digital and hands-on techniques to deliver stable, easy-to-wear prostheses — partials or full arches.

When patients choose removable dentures
  • Multiple missing teeth where implant options are delayed or unsuitable
  • Interim prosthesis following extractions
  • Cost-conscious full arch replacement

How we make your denture at Dentistica

Precise impressions → natural fit → confident smile

Our workflow blends digital planning and careful laboratory craftsmanship. Every denture is tried and tuned in the clinic until fit, occlusion and aesthetics are right.

1 — Assessment & Planning

Oral exam, ridge evaluation, study models and patient expectations discussion.

2 — Impression & Try-in

Accurate impressions, shade selection, and try-in appointment to confirm bite and look.

3 — Delivery & Adjustments

Final placement, comfort checks, and follow-up adjustments included.

Benefits
  • Cost-effective tooth replacement
  • Easy maintenance & repairable
  • Quick turnaround with modern workflows
How long until I receive my denture?

Timelines vary, but typical workflows span 2–4 weeks depending on try-in stages and lab work.

Are they comfortable?

Modern materials and careful fitting make them comfortable — we include adjustment visits to refine fit.

Skin PRP Treatment

Skin PRP (platelet-rich plasma) harnesses your own growth factors to stimulate collagen, improve texture and reduce fine lines. At Dentistica we pair PRP with microneedling or targeted injections using sterile, reproducible protocols for consistent, natural results.

Who benefits from Skin PRP
  • Fine lines and early skin laxity
  • Uneven skin texture and post-acne scarring
  • Those seeking natural collagen stimulation without fillers
Sessions
2–4 recommended
Downtime
24–72 hours mild redness
Results
Visible after 4–12 weeks
How Skin PRP is performed at Dentistica

Blood draw → PRP preparation → microneedling or targeted injections

We prepare a concentrated platelet-rich plasma from your blood and apply it via microneedling or small-volume injections to boost the skin's own repair mechanisms. Procedures are performed in sterile conditions with attention to comfort and predictable outcomes.

1 — Consultation & Skin Assessment

Evaluate skin concerns, set realistic goals and discuss combination options (microneedling, chemical peels, lasers).

2 — PRP Preparation

Blood draw and centrifuge in-clinic to obtain a high-quality PRP fraction.

3 — Treatment & Aftercare

Microneedling with PRP or targeted injections followed by simple post-care instructions to optimise recovery and results.

Benefits
  • Natural approach using patient's own biology (low risk of allergy)
  • Improves texture, tone, and scarring over time
  • Can be combined with other aesthetic procedures for enhanced outcomes
How many treatments are needed?

Usually 2–4 sessions spaced 4–6 weeks apart, then maintenance depending on response and skin goals.

Is PRP safe for the face?

Yes — since PRP uses the patient's own blood, allergic reactions are extremely rare. All procedures are performed with sterile technique.

When will I see results?

Initial improvements in texture may appear within weeks; collagen-driven changes become more noticeable after ~8–12 weeks.

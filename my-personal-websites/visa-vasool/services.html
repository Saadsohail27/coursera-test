Our Services

We go beyond test prep, essays, and activities. Our holistic approach helps you discover your passions, build leadership skills, and craft a compelling narrative that showcases your unique talents.

University Admissions Strategy

Visa Vasool students work with a dedicated Strategist to: build a school list tailored to their goals and strengths, strategically choose application deadlines, and ensure their application is tied together by a stand-out cohesive theme.

Extracurricular and Leadership Mentoring

Our Extracurricular Mentors help students build a robust extracurricular profile, including a Leadership (Capstone) Project that serves as the most important and impactful item on their Activities List.

Essay Writing Development

From ideation and outlining, through multiple rounds of revisions, to delivering polished final drafts, our Essay Mentors support students in crafting a personal essay, supplemental essays, and short answer questions that shine.

Academic Acceleration

Beyond helping students elevate their scores on the (Digital) SAT, ACT, and AP/IB exams, our academic tutors provide guidance on strategic course selection to ensure students meet the first admissions criteria for top colleges: academic excellence.

Research and Work Experience

Our students have access to college-level research programs that can lead to stand-out publishable papers. Our internship opportunities further provide students access to work experience programs at leading companies such as Uber, Amazon, Ferrari, The Economist, and more.

College Sport Recruitment

Student athletes are guided by former Ivy League and Division 1 coaches and athletes through the complex college recruiting process, including athlete development, coach outreach, recruitment video support, and more.
